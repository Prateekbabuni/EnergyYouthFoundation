About Our NGO

Energy Youth Foundation, established in 2012, works towards providing educational access and resources to children in India with the goal of breaking the cycle of poverty. They focus on literacy and financial assistance for education, building and supporting schools and students, and providing educational materials and scholarships.

They also offer career guidance and support for teachers and parents to ensure that children receive a proper education. The organization also advocates for the right to education for all children in India and offers scholarships and other support for students to aid in their future studies.
