About Energy Youth Foundation

Energy Youth Foundation has been registered under Society Reg. Act 1860 (Reg no. – 3379) from govt. of India. Energy Youth Foundation is an online/offline based platform, which aimed to provide easy & quick access to the quality education for students throughout the India. We provide proper guidance to the youth, which help them to achieve the height of sky. Our aim is to provide affordable & quality education to students, which transform them from a student to a confident learner and a great achiever. Our concern is to make the education affordable for all section of society. No matter what’s their financial condition. With this vision, to help the student of India we provide the best guidance to students. Energy Youth Foundation was started in 2011 and currently served over 1,00,000 students by our best guidance. Our experienced team conducts counselling in such way which is highly beneficial to the students in finding the best courses and best college for their bright future/career.

About Scholarship Test

Energy Youth Foundation conduct EYF scholarship test from last 5 years. The Scholarship test is based on a MCQ pattern which help students to be more practical & careful while attempting the questions. EYF scholarship test is not only help to identify & nurture the talent of students but also provide the scholarship of Rs 50,000 to Rs 5, 00,000 to students for higher education.
We will conduct EYF scholarship test all over India: Student Credit card facility is available in all three states:
  • In Bihar (Bihar Student Credit Card)
  • In Jharkhand (Jharkhand Student Credit Card)
  • In West Bengal(West Bengal Student Credit Card)

Benefits of Energy Youth Foundation Scholarship Test

  • -In this scholarship test we provide scholarship up to Rs 5,00,000 to the best performer of the test.
  • -After this scholarship student can also apply for Student credit card scheme from their respective state government. We will provide our 100% support and assistance in this process.
  • -By the help of EYF scholarship & Student credit card scheme, higher education of the students becomes more affordable.
  • -All of the colleges we recommend have the following recognitions: NAAC A+, NBA accreditation, and NIRF rating. This will create a great impact on your career.
  • -Counseling is entirely free. There is not a single rupee paid for any aspect of consultation.
  • -All categories like SC/ST/EBC/OBC/GEN/PH can take benefits of the EYF scholarship test.
  • -If a student is having trouble getting into a course, they can contact us directly. Our highly experienced team provides 100% support and assistance in whole process.
